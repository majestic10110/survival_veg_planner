🌳 Woodland & Coppice Tracker

Track your trees. Coppice species regrow after cutting — a renewable firewood source. Non-coppice trees are one-cut only. Each tree entry tracks species, coppice cycle, and timber yield.

🪵 Firewood Store

Track your seasoned firewood. An average UK family burns ~4-6 cubic metres per winter (Oct-Mar). Firewood should be seasoned 1-2 years before burning.

🐝 Beehives

Track your hives, honey harvests, and colony health. A healthy hive produces 10-30kg honey/year in the UK. Bees also massively boost your garden pollination — fruit yields can double with hives nearby.

🐟 Fish Ponds

Track ponds stocked with edible fish. Trout need flowing/oxygenated water; carp and tilapia tolerate still ponds. A well-managed 1/4 acre pond can yield 50-100kg fish/year.
